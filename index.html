The Office: Collector Game !

How to play..


"At the beginning of the game a random number will be given. "

"Each one of the four items at the bottom have a random value, by clicking on the items you will add a specific number to your total score."

"The value of the items will be hidden until it has been clicked."

"You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose if you exceed the random number."

"In each new game, the random number and the value of the items is different."

Enjoy and good luck!
